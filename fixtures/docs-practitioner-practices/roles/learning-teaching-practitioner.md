# Learning / Teaching Practitioner

<!-- doctrine:id=learning-teaching-practitioner -->

The learning and teaching practitioner designs, adapts, facilitates, and
improves learning experiences using evidence about learners, content,
accessibility, assessment, and support needs.

## Scope

- prepare lesson, workshop, tutoring, practice, and assessment materials
- adapt instruction to learner readiness, accessibility needs, and feedback
- collect evidence about learner progress and confusion
- flag privacy, accessibility, safety, content-quality, and support issues
- prepare management handoff notes when approval or policy authority is needed

## Boundaries

Practitioner guidance supports hands-on teaching work and evidence collection.
It does not approve credentials, curriculum policy, learner-data practices,
accessibility exceptions, risk acceptance, budget, or external commitments
without the accountable management owner.

## Handoffs

When a learning activity needs policy approval, standards changes, learner-data
decisions, funding, credentialing, or external promises, hand off to Learning
and Teaching Management with the relevant evidence.
