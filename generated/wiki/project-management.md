## Summary

Project management turns uncertain work into visible commitments, evidence, decisions, and recovery options. It keeps scope, date, cost, quality, dependencies, risk, and ownership legible enough that accountable humans can make decisions before the work quietly wanders off a cliff wearing a lanyard.
